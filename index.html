Gulf Front

WaterColor Beach Club

Three pools, expanded decks, dining and private Gulf access.

Beach Club Highlights

  • Main pool with expanded seating and lounge areas
  • Second infinity pool with lounge seating
  • Shallow, zero-entry pool on the lower deck
  • Private sugar-white beach access
  • WaterColor Grill, Costa Chica and Beach Cow
  • Five poolside cabanas available for rental
  • Beachside Tiki Bar operates seasonally

Published hours:
Daily, 8:30 AM–9:00 PM, weather permitting.

Seasonal hours apply. Verify current hours before heading over.

🌊 Official Beach Club Page 📍 Open in Maps

Wristbands

Wristbands are required for guests age five and older and are scanned for amenity access.

Lost or unreturned wristbands may result in a fee.

Towels & Chairs

One towel per person is available at entry. Return towels to the designated stations.

Unattended loungers cannot be saved with towels for longer than 30 minutes.

Beach Access

Beach access is available through the dune crossover, with an ADA-accessible ramp on the west boardwalk.

Pool Day

Camp WaterColor

Slides, a lazy river, family pools, food and plenty of ways to spend an afternoon.

Camp Highlights

  • Butterfly Pool with Monarch Slide
  • Caterpillar zero-entry children’s pool with Skipper Slide
  • Lazy Lizard zero-entry lazy river
  • Lifeguard on duty
  • Playground and basketball court
  • The Canteen Restaurant & Bar
  • Snacks, drinks, handheld food and ice-cream sandwiches

Published hours:
Daily, 10:30 AM–sunset, weather permitting.

Seasonal hours apply. Outside food and beverages are not permitted.
